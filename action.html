Action

action refers to sequences filled with intense physical activity, excitement, and dynamic events, such as fights, chases, explosions, or stunts. These scenes are designed to thrill and engage the audience, often forming the core of action-oriented genres.

united 93

A real-time account of the events on United Flight 93, one of the planes hijacked on September 11th, 2001 that crashed near Shanksville, Pennsylvania when passengers foiled the terrorist plot.

V for Vendetta

In a future British dystopian society, a shadowy freedom fighter, known only by the alias of "V", plots to overthrow the tyrannical government

The Last Samurai

In 19th century Japan, Nathan Algren, a US army captain, is hired by the Japanese emperor to train his army in the modern warfare techniques.
